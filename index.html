Milton's First Cat Cafe

Welcome to Milton's Cat Cafe!

Nestled in the heart of Milton, our Cat Cafe is a haven for cat lovers and coffee enthusiasts alike. At Milton's Cat Cafe, we believe in creating a warm, inviting space where you can unwind, enjoy a delicious cup of coffee, and spend quality time with our adorable feline friends.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring joy to our community through the companionship of cats and the comfort of a cozy cafe. We aim to provide a unique and memorable experience for our visitors, fostering connections between people and cats in a relaxing and friendly environment.

What We Offer

  • Delicious Beverages: Enjoy our carefully crafted selection of coffees, teas, and other beverages made with the finest ingredients.
  • Tasty Treats: Indulge in our assortment of pastries, snacks, and light bites, perfect for pairing with your drink of choice.
  • Adoptable Cats: All of our resident cats are available for adoption. If you fall in love with one of our furry friends, you can give them a forever home!
  • Cat-Themed Events: Join us for special events such as cat yoga, painting sessions, and more. Keep an eye on our events calendar for the latest happenings.
